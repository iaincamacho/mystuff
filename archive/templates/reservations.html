Terms and Conditions

Picnic Tables at Auborn Park (South) is a public space operated by the City of Bayview Parks and Recreation department. All payments are due the day of the reservation. Please call Greg Patterson (Parks and Recreation Asset Manager) if you have any questions. Your reserved space may be roped off to the public until your event. A park faculty manager will come to the location and remove the roping 15 minutes before your event. There is a $35 dollar clean up fee for not cleaning up after your event.
