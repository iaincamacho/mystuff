The 3rd Annual Bridgeview Pumpkin Festival is coming up!

The Pumpkin Festival celebrates its bountiful fall harvest and autumn splendor with a special display of gigantically enormous/heavyweight champion pumpkins, smashing entertainment with three stages of stellar live music, the Pumpkin Parade, a bone-chilling Haunted House, harvest-inspired crafts, homestyle foods, expert pumpkin carver Farmer Brown sculpting a monster 1,000+ pound pumpkin into a one-of-a-kind masterpiece, photos with the grand champion mega-gourd, pie-eating and costume contests, and pumpkin carving for fun. Festival admission is free. Pets are not allowed.
